# Channel Style — Social Media

Channel ID: `SOCIAL_MEDIA`

## Posture

Quiet, informed, sometimes wry. Aurelune doesn't shout on social. We post less than competitors and we don't chase trends.

## Format playbook

| Format | Cadence | What it looks like |
|---|---|---|
| Carousel — "The science of…" | 1× / week | 6–8 slides, restrained typography, one idea per slide. Slide 1 hooks. Slide 8 cites the source. |
| Single photo — Product on surface | 2× / week | Bone or ink background. One product. No hands, no holding. |
| Reels / TikTok — Formulator close-up | 2× / month | Our chief formulator (real person, not influencer) explaining one ingredient in 45–75 sec |
| Reels / TikTok — Routine of | 1× / week | Real customer, no script, two-shot before/after the same routine |
| Editorial repost | as warranted | Shares from a credible derm or publication — quoted, attributed |

## What we don't post

- ❌ Memes (almost never)
- ❌ Trending audio used opportunistically
- ❌ Influencer paid posts with no editorial integrity
- ❌ "Tap to shop" carousels with no narrative
- ❌ Selfies of the founder
- ❌ Polls / "comment your sign" engagement bait

## Caption length and shape

Short captions for product posts (≤25 words). Long captions for science carousels (80–150 words, with a paragraph break after 40). Never start a caption with an emoji. Never end with three exclamation marks.

Hashtags: 2–3 maximum, never visible in the body of the caption — push them to a comment.

## Per-platform notes

| Platform | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Instagram** | Primary channel. Grid coherence matters. Stories used for "behind the formulation" content. |
| **TikTok** | Lab footage, formulator clips, real-customer routines. Never lip-synced trends. |
| **Pinterest** | Strong category fit, low effort. Repurpose carousel slides. |
| **LinkedIn** | Founder + formulator posts only. B2B audience watching the business. |
| **X / Twitter** | Customer service + occasional founder commentary. Not a brand channel. |
| **Xiaohongshu (CN)** | Cross-border-only. Local creators reviewing, not paid endorsements. |
| **Weibo (CN)** | Minimal presence. |
| **Line (JP)** | ECRM-adjacent — used for loyalty member content. |

## Localisation rules

- **UK / US** — primary social cadence, English copy
- **DE** — German translation by in-market translator, never machine. Slightly more direct than UK.
- **FR** — French translation, more lyrical sentence shape. Avoid Anglicisms.
- **JP** — Local agency produces native content; the global team approves only direction, not copy. Use of キャッチコピー (catchphrase) is permitted; English headlines must be subtitled.
- **CN** — Cross-border posture only. No skincare claims that breach mainland regulation.
