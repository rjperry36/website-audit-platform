# Persona: Science Sven

> *"I want the INCI list, the percentages, the molecular weight if you've got it. Don't waste my time with lifestyle photography."*

**Archetype:** The ingredient researcher
**Age range:** 25–55
**Income band:** £40k–£120k (varies widely)
**Primary motivation:** Knowing exactly what's in the product and why

## Snapshot

Sven is the persona who keeps a spreadsheet of every product he's used, including INCI lists, prices per ml, and 4-week notes. He may be a chemist or engineer by trade, or he may just be that kind of person. He gravitates to brands that publish more, not less. He distrusts marketing copy and trusts citations.

He often discovers Aurelune through a Reddit thread or a Hacker News-adjacent skincare community rather than through paid media. He'll evangelise to his network once converted.

## What he buys

| Behaviour | Pattern |
|---|---|
| Categories | Actives — retinoids, peptides, acids, niacinamide, vitamin C |
| Price ceiling | Price-rational rather than price-capped. Will pay £120 if value is clear; refuses £30 if formulation is weak. |
| Replenishment cycle | Predictable — exactly when the last bottle runs out |
| Where he buys | D2C exclusively (after first verifying); occasional cross-border if a market gets a stronger formulation |

## What works on him

- Published white-papers
- "How we tested" deep-dive content
- Ingredient origin transparency (where the actives are sourced, who manufactures them)
- Formulator interviews on the blog
- Beta access to new products in exchange for detailed feedback

## What turns him off

- Vague claims, "powerful actives", "advanced formula"
- Photography-heavy PDPs with no scrollable detail
- Influencer marketing of any kind
- Discounts that imply margin was inflated to begin with

## Channel fit

| Channel | Affinity | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| `D2C` | Very high | Lives on the PDP and the science blog |
| `SEO` | Very high | Finds the brand via long-tail ingredient searches |
| `ECRM` | High | Opens "evidence update" emails, ignores "shop the look" |
| `RESEARCH` | Very high | Will join every consumer panel |
| `SOCIAL_MEDIA` | Low–Moderate | Reddit and LinkedIn, not Instagram |
| `EVENT` | High | Science panel, formulator Q&A, lab tour |

## Market spread

Strong in DE (matches the cultural disposition toward engineering precision), US, UK. Smaller cohort in FR and JP but the JP version reads research papers in three languages.
