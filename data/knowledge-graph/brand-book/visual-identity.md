# Aurelune — Visual Identity

## The mark

The Aurelune wordmark pairs with a circular "crescent" mark that references both **sun-set / moon-rise** (the AM/PM duality) and **the boundary of skin** (a closed protective surface).

| Lockup | When to use |
|---|---|
| **Primary lockup** (`logo-primary.svg`) | Wordmark + mark, horizontal. First choice for nearly everything. |
| **Wordmark only** (`logo-wordmark.svg`) | Where the mark won't scale (small footer, narrow OOH). |
| **Mark only** (`logo-mark.svg`) | App icon, social avatar, packaging cap emboss. Never use as the only brand cue on first impression. |

## Clear space and minimum size

- Clear space around the lockup = height of the "A" cap.
- Minimum print size = 18 mm wide for the primary lockup; 8 mm for the mark.
- Minimum digital size = 96 px wide for the primary lockup; 32 px for the mark.

## Colour

Restraint. Two neutrals do most of the work; a single accent carries warmth.

| Token | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| `aurelune.ink` | `#1B1B1F` | Body type, primary mark colour |
| `aurelune.bone` | `#F2EDE4` | Off-white background, wordmark on dark |
| `aurelune.dawn` | `#E8C9A3` | Accent — AM phase, warmth |
| `aurelune.dusk` | `#5C5A7A` | Accent — PM phase, depth |
| `aurelune.signal` | `#C84F2A` | Reserved for CTAs and warnings. Used sparingly. |
| `aurelune.mist` | `#D8D5CE` | Quiet utility — borders, dividers |

See `assets/palette.svg` for a visual reference. **Never** introduce a colour outside the palette without sign-off from the global brand team.

## Typography

Two type families. One for prose, one for moments.

| Family | Role |
|---|---|
| **Söhne** (or web equivalent: Inter) | Body, navigation, captions, UI |
| **Söhne Mono** | Lab notes, ingredient call-outs, product code, OOH headlines when a mono feel is wanted |
| **GT Sectra** | Editorial display only — long-form headlines, hero moments. Never below 24 pt. |

Tracking: tight on display, default on body. Leading: generous (1.4–1.5 on body).

## Photography

- **Product**: still life. Single light source, soft shadow. Bone or ink backgrounds only.
- **Lifestyle**: real adults, no Photoshop on skin. Even contact-sheet feel — not pristine. Diverse skin tones, age ranges, gender expressions. Shot in domestic settings (a bathroom shelf, a bedside table).
- **Ingredient close-ups**: macro. Hero a single texture. No spoons-of-serum-being-poured shots.

## Layout

- **Generous whitespace.** Always.
- **Single-column hierarchy** on most touchpoints — headline, supporting line, evidence, action.
- **Grid**: 12-column digital, 9-column print, 4-column mobile. 8 px base.
- **Asymmetric balance** is preferred over centred symmetry.

## What never appears

- ❌ Gold foil
- ❌ Cursive script type
- ❌ Stock photography
- ❌ Models holding the product up to their face
- ❌ The word "luxury" in headlines
- ❌ Any colour outside the palette
- ❌ More than two type families on a single asset
